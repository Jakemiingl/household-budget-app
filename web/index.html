All accounts

Enter the APR and your typical monthly payment on credit cards & loans (works for Plaid accounts too — sync won't overwrite them). The payment is what lets us model real principal paydown for an accurate payoff date and true interest.

Debt payoff plan

Avalanche method — putting extra money toward the highest-APR debt first clears your debt for the least total interest. With a monthly payment set, payoff dates and interest are amortized (interest shrinks as the balance falls), not a flat balance × rate.

Possible rule mismatches

Transactions a rule filed under a category whose kind disagrees with Plaid's own category — e.g. a rule called it spending but Plaid says it's a transfer. Review and fix the ones that are wrong.

Add a rule

Create a keyword rule directly — no uncategorized transaction needed. "Applies to" lets the same keyword route by direction (e.g. mercury → Debt Payment when money out, Income when money in).

Add a category

Create a new category to file transactions under (e.g. Business Expense). Kind drives the math: income counts as money in, expense as spending, transfer is excluded from both.

Connect a bank (Plaid)

Add your Plaid keys to .env first (see the steps below). Then connect your real bank, or — in Sandbox — connect a fake test bank instantly.

Chat from your phone (Telegram)

  1. In Telegram, message @BotFather, send /newbot, follow the prompts, and copy the bot token.
  2. Put it in .env as TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN= and restart the app.
  3. Open your new bot in Telegram and send it any message — it replies with your chat ID.
  4. Add that ID (and your spouse's) to TELEGRAM_ALLOWED_CHAT_IDS= (comma-separated) in .env and restart.
  5. Now you can both ask things like "Can we afford a $2,000 couch?" from your phones — even while out shopping (the app must be running on this PC).

Getting Plaid keys

  1. Sign up free at dashboard.plaid.com.
  2. Open Developers → Keys; copy your client_id and the Sandbox secret.
  3. Copy .env.example to .env, paste them in, keep PLAID_ENV=sandbox, and restart the app.
  4. Click Connect your bank and use Plaid's test login user_good / pass_good to verify the flow.
  5. For real accounts: request Production access in the dashboard, swap in the Production secret, and set PLAID_ENV=production.
